WHAT IS BALLON D'OR?

The Ballon d'Or is an annual football award presented by France Football. It has been awarded since 1956. The Ballon d'Or award honours the best player over the previous year. It is based on football journalists votes.

MESSI AND ONLY MESSI

Messi started winning the award 2009. He won it 4 years in a row till 2012. Then he won it again 2015. This year he has won the sixth and is now the player with most Ballon D'Ors in history.
